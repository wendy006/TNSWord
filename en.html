1.READING

1.1.HOME

  • Tapping any of the cells to enter an detail page
  • Search icon in upper right is there for word searching

1.2.DETAIL

  • Tapping the button in the bottom right,which will trigger the current chapter to play.
  • long Tapping on the chapter word will trigger the basic info of this word,and then tap the top bar will drawback the whole bottom view.this rect is allowed to be dragged up to the top and then to search the custom word.

    (NOTE:"add"operation in the word info box will save it to the local database and in the meantime,it will sender the word to shanbay server which will allow you to deeply learn it lately.)

  • The upper right button here is pressed to enter the chinese translation page,
  • Dragging the left edge of the page could also turn back to last page.
  • Every reading chapter's READING RECORDwill be automatically generated or updated.

2.INTENSIVE STUDY

2.1.HOME

  • Tap the tip view in the middle or the bar view above middle and then choose one of the sentence in the drop-down box. it will show specific info below when you have done this.

    (NOTE: Chapter index and sentence index are both needed ,chapter without its specific sentence index is invalid which can not produce the target effect)

  • We can firstly see the chinese translation of the current sentence,by which we may try to translate the sentence into english.
  • Tap the translation label to drawback it,then we may press the upper left button to play the audio to check your writing sentence. Or we then, can tap the game button to start a related sentence game. GAME PAGE

2.1.DETAIL

  • The game rule: Putting every word of sentence to the correct place,and finally this words would be linked into an right sentence. Every sentence's GAME RECORD will be automatically generated or updated.

    （NOTE:Currently,the game page is perfectly fit for devices of 320px screen width,other sorts of devices can also work well,but in aesthetic aspects,not that much.）

  • The word boxes can be moved by gestures(drag)or tap.The correct rate can be updated dynamically in the upper right.Every operation's feedback can be received by sounds and color of boxes.
  • Voice button in bottom left can mostly play the sentence audio twice, however, translation button is allow to show limitless times.

3.WORD BOOK

  • The word book is seperated into three parts : 1.unfamiliar words 2.familiar words 3. whole words. After the initial "add word"operation, the specifical word is come to unfamiliar box ,words in which should be studied frequently, then if needed. you can move the familiar words into another box by switch the second button in the table cell.

    HOW TO STUDY IN WORD BOOK :

    1).firstly look at the word,if we can easily classify it into easy-word, then move it to "familiar box",moreover,if we think it is no necessary to put it to the word book,then just long tap the word and choose to delete in the confirmation box.

    2).if we can not remember the word,we can tap the "letter" picture in the bottom of the cell to see the english translation, if this operation can neither solve the problem, then tap the word to enter the word detail page.

4.SETTING

  • Open or close the sound-effect inside TNSWord application. we can also control the text fontsize of READING PAGE
  • Check the records of reading and game of intensive study.
  • Jump to shanbay application or website
  • Clear the cache or log out.

    NOTE:logging out will remove local word book info and simultaneously disable user to add word to their shanbay account

5.ABOUT ME

  • TNSWord application's development is based in SHAN BAY API
  • TNSWord's reading content is from 《 New Concept English 4》.
